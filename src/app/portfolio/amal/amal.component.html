History

Couple of months ago I was called by the administrative people of AMAL College, who got my contacts through a friend of mine, regarding website development for their college. They said they need to revamp the entire site from groundup and should look professional it is one the requirement for NAAC Accreditation.

Project Description

The website is similar to any educational institute with important features such as History of the Institute, Details of the Management and Staff, Courses Offered, Facilities of the College, Alumni Registration, etc.
